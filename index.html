ℹ️ About USMLE Medico

USMLE Medico is a free, community-driven USMLE preparation platform built specifically for Nepali and South Asian medical students who are preparing for the United States Medical Licensing Examination.

Our platform aggregates the world's best USMLE preparation resources — including PDFs, notes, video lectures from top educators, and recommended guides — organized by subject and step so you can find everything in one place without endless searching.

"I built this platform because I knew how hard it was to find organized, reliable USMLE resources as a student in Nepal. Everything here is what I wish I had from the beginning." — username, Founder of USMLE Medico

All resources linked from this platform are official external platforms (Pathoma, UWorld, AMBOSS, Sketchy, Boards & Beyond, OnlineMedEd, Divine Intervention). We do not host copyrighted material — we organize and guide you to the best sources.
